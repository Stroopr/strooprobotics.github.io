Analytics & Support

Seldon is a Virtual Financial Assistant. He is a member of the finance team preforming tasks related to Financial Reporting and Analysis (FRA). FRA is the process of building and analysing company's financial statements for decision-making purposes.

Seldon has two main functions:

  • Seldon Analytics

    Seldon provides insightful financial reports and analysis to the management.

  • Seldon Support

    Seldon helps financial controllers along the FRA process from the collection of financial data to their treatment and their dissemination.
